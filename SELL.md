# Sell Aurora Rayes Books Agent

## Positioning
Aurora Rayes Books Agent is an Aurora Rayes ecosystem product for readers, book clubs, coaches, educators, and content teams. It turns books workflows into a guided AI experience that can be sold as a subscription, bundled offer, or managed-service accelerator.

## Ideal customer profile
- Users actively trying to improve books outcomes.
- Coaches, consultants, agencies, or educators who need a branded AI workflow.
- Teams that want a lightweight assistant before investing in a custom platform.

## Outcomes to sell
- Book recommendations
- Reading summaries
- Discussion questions
- Reading tracker
- Author insights

## Demo talk track
1. Open the landing page and explain the Aurora Rayes ecosystem fit.
2. Show the dashboard assistant and the first three ready states.
3. Walk through pricing placeholders and explain checkout is disabled until approved Stripe IDs are configured.
4. Close with a custom onboarding or bundle offer.

## Packaging placeholders
| Package | Placeholder | Intended buyer |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| Starter | Replace with approved launch price | Individuals validating the workflow |
| Pro | Replace with approved subscription price | Power users and small teams |
| Partner | Replace with approved custom quote | Agencies, coaches, and portfolio bundles |

## Sales assets to prepare
- One landing-page hero screenshot.
- A 60-second demo video.
- Three outcome-based testimonials or beta quotes.
- Approved Stripe product and Price IDs stored only in deployment secrets.

No live payment links are included. Do not add checkout URLs or live Stripe keys to source control.
